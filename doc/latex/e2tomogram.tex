\section{e2tomogram.py} \index{Program Manuals!e2tomogram.py} \label{e2tomogram.py}
\subsection{Usage}
usage: e2tomogram.py [options] input\_stack.hed output.hed


\subsection{Description}
Processes a tomographic tilt series




\subsection{Options}
